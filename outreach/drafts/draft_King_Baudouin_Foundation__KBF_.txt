Dear Grants Officer,

On behalf of NGO "Kosmos Tabir," I am writing to present a high-impact, immediate-relief project that restores energy security to community infrastructure in active combat and de-occupied zones in Ukraine.

While utility reconstruction takes years, critical public services in regions like Kharkiv, Kherson, and Zaporizhzhia remain in total darkness. Local medical clinics (FAPs) cannot power refrigeration or emergency devices, village councils cannot coordinate aid, and local kindergartens are closed.

We resolve this by installing off-grid solar stations on key public buildings. We pair funding with donated, decommissioned solar panels (80%+ efficiency) from the EU. This model is exceptionally cost-effective:

* **$10,000** covers the complete solar electrification of one frontline settlement — village council, medical outpost, and local kindergarten, establishing a reliable local energy grid.
* Sponsoring a region establishes decentralized energy security for local clinics and emergency resilience points serving thousands of citizens.

We have already successfully electrified 40+ public buildings and deployed 400+ micro-solar installations. Our operations are officially coordinated with the Ministry of Energy of Ukraine.

**We are seeking funding partners to help us electrify 20 more frontline communities for the upcoming winter.**

I have attached our detailed presentation, budget outline, and project roadmap. I would love the opportunity to introduce our project and discuss how King Baudouin Foundation (KBF) / your foundation can support this campaign.
