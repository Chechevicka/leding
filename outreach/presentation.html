The Context

War Destroyed the Grid. Communities in Blackout.

Infrastructure Collapse

Relentless military strikes on power stations have collapsed the grid across large zones of East and South Ukraine. Critical public facilities are left without power.

  • 475+ settlements completely cut off from power.
  • Local administration & clinics are unable to coordinate aid or provide basic emergency care.
  • Schools & Kindergartens are closed, disrupting children's education and community stability.

"Without grid power, decentralized solar on key public buildings is the only way to sustain local emergency services, communication, and basic medical care."

- Ministry of Energy of Ukraine
Our Solution

Solar for Critical Public Infrastructure

We install complete, off-grid solar power systems on key public buildings to ensure essential services continue working through blackouts and winter.

Village Councils & Admin Offices

Keeping the administrative heart of the community alive to coordinate humanitarian aid and emergency services.

Medical Outposts (FAPs) & Kindergartens

Securing power for local healthcare, diagnostics, and providing a warm, illuminated environment for children.

Resilient Design Features

  • Stable Winter Setup: Vertically-mounted panels to maximize winter solar angles and prevent snow accumulation.
  • New, Local Batteries: We only install new, locally procured battery systems for maximum safety and warranty. **No used batteries are imported.**
  • Resilience Points: Establishing public charging hubs ("Points of Invincibility") where residents power phones, power banks, and walkie-talkies.
Field Verification

Resiliency in Action: Beryslav Community Hub

The Shrapnel Test

In August 2024, a guided aerial bomb detonated near a community building where we had installed a solar station.

  • Direct hit: The solar panels were hit by shrapnel and punctured with multiple holes.
  • Operational status: The system kept working. The panels retained over 50% of their starting efficiency, keeping the clinic and hub powered.
  • Proven Resiliency: Distributed solar installations survive combat shelling that completely takes down centralized power grids.

The Circular Economy

Europe's Solar Surplus is Ukraine's Lifeline

Millions of solar panels are retired or decommissioned across the European Union every year due to contract upgrades or cosmetic wear.

High Recycling Cost

Crushing panels in the EU is expensive and energy-intensive. Many functional panels sit in warehouses for years.

Immediate Humanitarian Second Life

For a clinic or local authority hub, a panel at 80% efficiency is a vital source of electricity for medical refrigeration and communication.

Logistical Fit

NGO "Kosmos Tabir" offers a direct, eco-friendly solution for European solar companies:

  • We accept decommissioned panels (200W+, min. 80%+ efficiency).
  • We coordinate customs clearance and waivers under humanitarian cargo regulations.
  • We seek logistics partnerships to assist with shipping from your EU warehouse.
  • Full verification reports (GPS, photos, signatures) are shared with your ESG board.
